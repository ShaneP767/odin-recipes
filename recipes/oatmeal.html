Oatmeal

Manna from the Gods

A simple classic that hits the spot, breakfast or a snack.


Here's what youll need.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of dry oats
  • 3/4 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 cup milk

  • Optional

    • Dried Fruit
    • 1 Tbsp peanut butter
    • 1 tsp instant coffee
    • 1/4 tsp cinnamon

Steps

  1. Step 1

    Add Oats, Sugar and milk into a microwave safe bowl.

  2. Step 2

    Mix well and microwave for 30 seconds

  3. Step 3

    Stir and let sit for 15 seconds

  4. Step 4

    Microwave another 30 seconds, stir and let sit for 30 seconds

  5. Optional

    • Stir in peanut butter for a protein boost
    • Mix in that tsp of coffee for that morning pick me up
    • Top with dried fruits for a vitamin boost
    • Mix in the cinnamon for an unique flavor

This recipe is perfect for a quick hearty snack and can be easily transformed into a gourmet experience with a little imagination.
