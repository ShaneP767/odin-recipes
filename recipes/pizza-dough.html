Home made pizza dough

The secret sauce is in the dough

An often overlooked part of a good pizza is the dough but done right.

it'll have you craving more thatn just the delicious toppings!


Here's what you'll need.


Ingredients

  • 1 cup warm water(110f/45c)
  • 1 (.25 ounce) package active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar
  • 2 Â½ cups bread flour
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Step 1


    Gather all ingredients. Preheat oven to 450f(230c), and lightly grease a pizza pan.

  2. Step 2


    Place warm water in a bowl;add yeast and sugar. Mix and let stand until creamy, about 10 minutes

  3. Step 3


    Add flour, oil, and salt to the yeast mixture; beat until smooth.
    You can do this by hand or use a stand mixer fitted with a dough hook to make it easier.

  4. Step 4


    Let rest for 5 minutes.

  5. Step 5


    Turn dough out onto a lightly floured surface and pat or roll into a 12-inch circle.

  6. Step 6


    Transfer to the prepared pizza pan.
